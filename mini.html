About Mini Miltiya

Doodle Army 2: Mini Militia is a 2D multiplayer action game that challenges you to face opponents from all over the world.
There's even the option to join in with up to six different players logged on simultaneously during online battles via mobile. Gameplay is super user-friendly: the virtual joystick that helps you move your character is located on the left side of the screen and the aiming stick is to the right. Your weapon shoots automatically as long as you're pointing at your target. So, all you have to do is worry about aiming. Each scene is fairly open-ended, there's lots of space for you to explore.
